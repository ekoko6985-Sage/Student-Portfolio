Who I Am

Hello! I'm Emmanuel Koko, a passionate cybersecurity student at Miva Open University, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree. I am deeply committed to understanding and protecting digital systems, networks, and data from evolving cyber threats.

As a first-year student, I'm building a solid foundation in cybersecurity principles, ethical hacking, network security, programming, and digital forensics. My goal is to develop the knowledge and practical skills needed to become a competent cybersecurity professional who can help secure organizations against cyber risks.

With a background that includes specialized training in Digital Film Animation, I bring a creative perspective to problem-solving and visual communication of complex security concepts.
